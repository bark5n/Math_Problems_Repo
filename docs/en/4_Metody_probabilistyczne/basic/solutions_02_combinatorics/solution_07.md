## Task 7 — k-Permutations (Ordered Selections)

Here order matters and repetition is not allowed.

---

### 1. Assign first three places among 12 runners

Number of possibilities:

P(12,3) = 12 × 11 × 10

---

### 2. 4-digit numbers with distinct digits from 1–9

Choose and order 4 digits:

P(9,4)

---

### 3. Numbers divisible by 5

Last digit must be 5.

Remaining positions:

Choose 3 digits from remaining 8:

P(8,3)
