### Task 3 Answer

**Sample space:**  
\(\Omega = \{(4,0), (4,1), (4,2), (5,0), (5,1), (5,2), (6,0), (6,1), (6,2)\}\)  
Each outcome has probability \(1/9\).

---

1. **Job completed in 4 hours:**  
Favorable outcomes: \((4,0), (4,1), (4,2)\)  
\[
P = 3/9 = 1/3
\]

2. **Job completed flawlessly in 6 hours:**  
Favorable outcome: \((6,0)\)  
\[
P = 1/9
\]

3. **Job completed in at most 5 hours:**  
Favorable outcomes: all with 4 or 5 hours:  
\((4,0),(4,1),(4,2),(5,0),(5,1),(5,2)\)  
\[
P = 6/9 = 2/3
\]

4. **Job completed in at most 5 hours and ≤1 error:**  
Favorable outcomes: \((4,0),(4,1),(5,0),(5,1)\)  
\[
P = 4/9
\]
