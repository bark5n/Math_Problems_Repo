## Task 3 — Permutations with Repeated Elements

When identical symbols appear, we divide by factorials of the repeated counts.

---

### 1. MISSISSIPPI

Letter counts:

M = 1  
I = 4  
S = 4  
P = 2

Total letters = 11

Number of distinct arrangements:

11! / (4! · 4! · 2!)

This formula removes overcounting caused by identical letters.

---

### 2. STATISTICS

Letter counts:

S = 3  
T = 3  
I = 2  
A = 1  
C = 1

Total letters = 10

Number of arrangements:

10! / (3! · 3! · 2!)

---

### 3. Arrangements of STATISTICS starting with S

Fix S in the first position.

Remaining letters:

S = 2  
T = 3  
I = 2  
A = 1  
C = 1

Remaining letters = 9

Number of arrangements:

9! / (2! · 3! · 2!)
