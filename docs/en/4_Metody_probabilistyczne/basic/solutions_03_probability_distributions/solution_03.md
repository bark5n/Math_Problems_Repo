## Task 3 — Geometric Model (Waiting for First Event)

### Random Experiment

We observe pages until the first error appears.

---

### Sample Space

Ω = {1,2,3,...}

---

### Probability Distribution

P(X = k) = (1−p)^(k−1) · p

---

### Success Definition

Success = occurrence of the first error

---

### Key Idea

We count trials until first success
