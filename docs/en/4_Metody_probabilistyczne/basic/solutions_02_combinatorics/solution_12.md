## Task 12 — Mixed Counting Problems

### 1. Student ID Codes

Two letters (A–E) + three digits.

Repetition allowed:

5² × 10³

No repetition in letters:

P(5,2) × 10³

No repetition anywhere:

P(5,2) × P(10,3)

---

### 2. Medal Assignment

Three medals among 12 runners:

P(12,3)

If two particular runners must receive medals:

Choose medals for them and one more runner.

---

### 3. Committee Selection

Committee of 4 from 10:

C(10,4)

Exactly two women:

C(4,2) × C(6,2)

At least one woman:

C(10,4) − C(6,4)

---

### 4. Circular Seating

Seven people around table:

6!

Two particular people together:

5! × 2!

---

### 5. Passwords

Characters from 36 symbols.

Repetition allowed:

36⁵

No repetition:

P(36,5)
