## Task 4 — Circular Permutations

In circular arrangements, rotations are considered identical.  
Therefore we fix one position and arrange the remaining people.

---

### 1. Seating 7 people around a round table

Fix one person to remove rotational symmetry.

Number of arrangements:

(7 − 1)! = 6!

---

### 2. Two particular people must sit next to each other

Treat the two people as one block.

Now we arrange:

6 objects around the circle.

Circular arrangements:

(6 − 1)! = 5!

Inside the block the two people can swap places:

2!

Total arrangements:

5! × 2!

---

### 3. Two particular people must sit opposite each other

Fix one of the two people.

The second person must sit exactly opposite.

Remaining 5 people can be arranged freely:

5!
