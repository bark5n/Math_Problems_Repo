## Task 4 — Poisson Model (Arrival of Events)

### Random Experiment

We count the number of error reports in one hour.

---

### Sample Space

Ω = {0,1,2,3,...}

---

### Probability Distribution

P(X = k) = (e^(−λ) · λ^k) / k!

---

### Parameter

λ = 3 (average number per hour)

---

### Key Idea

Poisson models rare events over time
