## Task 5 — Multinomial Model

### Random Experiment

Roll a die 5 times and classify outcomes.

---

### Categories

- small (1–2) → 1/3  
- medium (3–4) → 1/3  
- large (5–6) → 1/3  

---

### Distribution

P = 5! / (x₁! x₂! x₃!) · (1/3)^5  

---

### Parameters

x₁ + x₂ + x₃ = 5  

---

### Key Idea

Multiple categories → multinomial
