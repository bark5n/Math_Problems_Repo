## Task 1 — Binomial Model (Quality Control)

### Random Experiment

We inspect 3 consecutive screws.  
Each screw can be either good or defective.

---

### Sample Space

Ω = {GGG, GGD, GDG, DGG, GDD, DGD, DDG, DDD}

(G = good, D = defective)

---

### Probabilities

Let p = probability of defective.

Example:

P(GGD) = (1−p)(1−p)p  

Each outcome follows:

P = p^(number of D) · (1−p)^(number of G)

---

### Success Definition

Success = a defective screw

---

### Key Idea

This is a binomial model:
- fixed number of trials (3)
- independent
- same probability p
